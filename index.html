Exhibit — A Query, Worked

-- Which customer regions are worth the marketing budget? SELECT ROUND(AVG(rev_per_customer), 2) AS avg_lifetime_value, a.district FROM ( SELECT customer_id, SUM(amount) AS rev_per_customer FROM payment GROUP BY customer_id ) AS subquery INNER JOIN customer cu ON cu.customer_id = subquery.customer_id INNER JOIN address a ON a.address_id = cu.address_id GROUP BY a.district ORDER BY avg_lifetime_value DESC;

∴ Result

Ranks every district by average customer lifetime value — turning a payments table into a ranked shortlist a marketing team can act on this week, not next quarter.
